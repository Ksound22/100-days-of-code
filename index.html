Eusebio

Also known as Black Panther, the greatest ever footballer from Africa, arguably the greatest footballer of all time.

Below's the timeline of Eusebio's life

  • 1942 - Born in Loureço Marques, Portuguese Mozambique
  • 1960 - Arrived Portugal from Mozambique to start playing for Benfica
  • 1961 - Made his debut for Benfica, scoring three goals (hat-trick) in a 4-2 win
  • 1962 - Won Taça de Portugal and European Cup with Benfica, scoring two goals against Real Madrid in the final of the latter competition
  • 1962 - Finished second in the Balon D'or
  • 1963 - Selected to represent The FIFA team in the "Golden Anniversary" of The FA at Wembley Stadium
  • 1965 - Got married to Flora Claudina Burheim, Won The Balon D'or
  • 1966 - Won the FIFA World Cup Bronze medal with Portugal, also won the FIFA World Cup Golden Boot with 9 goals
  • 1968 - Became the first ever player to win The European Golden Boot
  • 1970 - Won The Portuguese Top Division Highest Goal Scorer Award(Also did the same in '64, '65 '66, '67, '68, amd '73
  • 1975 - Moved to The NASL to play for Boston Minutemen, Toronto Metros-Croatia and Las Vegas Quicksilvers
  • 1979 - Retired from active football
  • 2003 - Selected as Golden Player of Portugal by The Portuguese Football Federation
  • 2014 - Died, aged 71

Without doubt, Eusebio was one of the finest players i ever had the privilege to play against. Not only that, he was a true sportsman, as he proved in applauding Alex Stepney for his save in the European Cup final. I feel proud to have been both an opponent and friend and am saddened to hear of his passing. My thoughts and prayers are with his family at this difficult time.
